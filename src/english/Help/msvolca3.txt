\b;Walkthrough
1) Power a \l;legged shooter\u object\botfs;. There is even a \l;nuclear cell\u object\atomic; left behind the \l;bot factory\u object\factory;.

2) Climb the northern cliff and kill a few \l;ants\u object\ant;. The best access is from the east.

3) Keep going until all ants are out of the way.

4) Climb the northern cliff with a \l;legged grabber\u object\botgs; and retrieve the \l;black box\u object\bbox; that you will find in the derelict bot factory.
