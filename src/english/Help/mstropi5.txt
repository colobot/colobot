\b;Walkthrough
1) Locate the \l;black box\u object\bbox; on your own. You can try to spot it from above, using your jet engine, or dive into the water.
2) Build a \l;converter\u object\convert; near the chunks of titanium ore. You'll find them in the vicinity of the spaceship to the east.
3) Produce two \l;cubes\u object\titan;.
4) Build a \l;bot factory\u object\factory; not too far from the black box.
5) Manufacture a \l;subber\u object\botsub;.
6) Bring the black box back on board.
7) Take off.

The black box is lying at the bottom of a small lake, northeast of the spaceship.

You can build the bot factory right beside the lake, on the south bank.
