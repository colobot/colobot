\b;Walkthrough
1) Build a \l;bot factory\u object\factory;.
2) Produce a \l;winged grabber\u object\botgj;.
3) Carry a \l;titanium cube\u object\titan; to the highest point.
4) Build a \l;radar\u object\radar; on the spot.
5) Build a \l;research center\u object\research; next to the spaceship.
6) Initiate the <defense tower research program> \button 68;.
7) Build the \l;defense tower\u object\tower; next to the radar.
8) When all the worms are destroyed, you can go back to your ship and take off.
