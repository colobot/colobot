\b;\button 162; Converter
\image convert 8 8;
This building was designed to convert chunks of \l;titanium ore\u object\titanore; into usable \l;titanium cubes\u object\titan;. All \l;you\u object\human; need to do is place a chunk at the center of the platform and step back. The converter takes care of the rest.

\t;Requirements for building
Any flat surface of at least 6 meters wide.

\t;Input
Titanium ore.

\t;Output
Titanium.

\t;Power source
None.

\t;Duration of operation
15 seconds.
