\b;Derelict bot
A derelict bot is what's left of a bot after it has been disabled by a \l;spider\u object\spider; or by friendly fire.

A \l;recycler\u object\botrecy; can convert a derelict bot back into a reusable \l;titanium cube\u object\titan;.
