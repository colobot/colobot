\b;Titanium
\image titan 4 4;
Titanium is indispensable for the construction of buildings, \l;bots\u object\factory; and \l;regular power cells\u object\energy;. There is usually some left on the \l;spaceship\u object\base; when you embark on a new mission.

If more is needed, you'll have to \l;convert\u object\convert; chunks of \l;titanium ore\u object\titanore; into usable titanium cubes.
