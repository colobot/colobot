\b;\button 172; Information Exchange Post
\image info 8 8;
This building stores digital information. A post can contain up to 10 pieces of information, each one referenced by a name.
For example, a post can contain 3 informations¦:\c;
\tab;"Position.x"  23.45
\tab;"Position.y"  -102.70
\tab;"Quantity"    3.00
\n;
To put new information, you must execute the command \c;\l;send\u cbot\send;\n;.
To read information from a post, you must execute the command \c;\l;receive\u cbot\receive;\n;.

\t;Requirements for building
Any flat surface of at least 2.5 meters wide.

\t;Input
None.

\t;Output
Digital information.

\t;Power source
None.

\t;Duration of operation
1 second.
