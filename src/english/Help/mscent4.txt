\b;Walkthrough
1) Search the area for \l;titanium\u object\titan;.
2) Build a \l;converter\u object\convert;.
3) Build a \l;research center\u object\research;.
4) Run the <thumper research program> \button 66;.
5) Manufacture a \l;thumper\u object\bottump;.
6) Position the thumper close to the derelict spaceship and "thump" the ground.
7) Quickly retrieve the \l;black box\u object\bbox; and bring it back aboard your \l;spaceship\u object\base;.
8) Bring the thumper along and take off.
